TIBCO Enterprise Message ServiceTM is a standards-based enterprise messaging platform that unifies existing systems, open standards, and leading technologies in IT on a common enterprise backbone and manages the real-time flow of information. A Java Message Service (JMS) 1.1-compliant product, TIBCO Enterprise Message Service enables enterprise-wide reusable services across different development environments and platforms with native support for leading Java 2 Platform, Enterprise Edition (J2EE)-based application servers, for Microsoft .NET on servers, desktops and mobile devices, and for C/C++ and COBOL on a variety of platforms including the mainframe. TIBCO's messaging software powers the communications infrastructure for the world's top financial institutions including NASDAQ, SWIFT, Morgan Stanley, Lehman Brothers, and Merrill Lynch, and is the foundation of TIBCO's event-driven and service-oriented architecture that is deployed by thousands of TIBCO customers worldwide.
