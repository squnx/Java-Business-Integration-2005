Trace has been delivering reliable solutions to the financial services sector for over 25 years and has been listed on the London Stock Exchange since 1989. We support some of the world's leading financial institutions who run mission critical applications and infrastructures that have been developed and implemented by Trace. Trace's software solutions empower our customers to run their infrastructures in complete confidence. Underpinning our success are our years of experience in the middleware, integration and messaging marketplace and our commitment to customers. These values enable us to understand client requirements and offer value to both business and technology streams through the deployment of first-class solutions, services and support. From a technology perspective alone, the issues associated with message mediation are generally not being properly addressed. The problems are normally both architectural and infrastructure-based. Service-orientation, event-driven architectures, enterprise service buses, standards-based infrastructures, are all key here. Therefore, Trace, working with our partners like Sun Microsystems, understands the issues and the need to offer real value to our customers. Key to messaging integration is the need to fulfill the promise of reuse, cost reduction, improved time-to-market, STP (straight-through-processing) and more, in terms of generating business services that remove pains in the short-term and deliver tangible benefit going forward.
