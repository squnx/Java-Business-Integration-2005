As a leading independent provider of BPEL solutions, the development tools and servers we offer provide full support for the JavaTM Business Integration specification. Our products deliver unrivaled platform portability and the most flexible, open architecture available today. The solutions help drive productivity and efficiency for our customers, through their systems, and with their extended communities.

The ActiveWebflowTM suite of products natively implements Business Process Execution Language (BPEL), the Web services standard for process orchestration and management. The solutions are available through open source and commercial licensing configurations.

Active Endpoints' products and services are used by organizations when they need to achieve a variety of system and business integration objectives, and have chosen to embrace the open standards of Web services and services-oriented architectures. ActiveWebflow ensures organizations significantly reduce costs, time and the risks of deploying services-oriented integrations, which allow our customers to:

- Leverage the evolving foundation of Web services standards to create nimble, responsive information systems
- Orchestrate robust cross-applications and cross-organization process flows
- Deploy and manage complex service-oriented integrations on a broad range of enterprise platforms
- Scale the use of services-oriented applications within the enterprise as well as across customer and partner communities

Customers in telecommunications, financial services, retail, distribution, manufacturing, and multiple U.S. government agencies have successfully implemented solutions using ActiveWebflow products.
