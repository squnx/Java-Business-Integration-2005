Cape Clear 6 is the latest version of Cape Clear Software's Enterprise Service Bus (ESB).

Cape Clear 6 is a standards-based enterprise service bus (ESB) that radically simplifies the integration of applications and data. It provides the tools and infrastructure to support the development and delivery of new services to your customers and partners. Cape Clear 6 offers the first end-to-end, 100 percent Web services-based enterprise service bus, spanning service creation through sophisticated service orchestration with native BPEL support.
