TIBCO© BusinessWorks provides a standards-based platform for rapidly developing, deploying and continuously improving services and integration processes. With TIBCO BusinessWorks, users can easily integrate data, semantics, and business logic across incompatible applications using popular standards such as Web services, and can orchestrate services that take advantage of those applications and information. Through a single integrated design environment users can configure application interfaces, model and orchestrate integration process flows, transform and map data formats, and test and deploy integration projects without having to write code. As a component of the real-time backbone, TIBCO BusinessWorks facilitates rapid enterprise deployments, mission-critical RAS and security, and enterprise management and monitoring for complete lifecycle management of enterprise integration. TIBCO BusinessWorks provides a foundation on which companies can build a service-oriented and event-driven architecture.
